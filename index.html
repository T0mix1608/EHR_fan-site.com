Welcome to the Endless Host Roles Website!

Endless Host Roles (EHR), the largest host-only mod for Among Us, was created by the ingenious Gurge44. Initially named TOHE Plus (TOHE+), as it was a modification of TOHE (Town Of Host), the mod now features significantly more roles and options than its first version, and Gurge continues to update it regularly.

About EHR:

EHR is the most extensive host-only mod for Among Us, featuring over 350 roles and 7 game modes. It also includes a Custom Team Assigner application to fully personalize your gaming experience!
